How ILF Neurofeedback Supports Mental Health

Understanding the impact of Infra-Low Frequency Neurofeedback on mental well-being

What is ILF Neurofeedback?

ILF Neurofeedback is a specialized form of brain training that focuses on very slow brain waves, operating below 0.1Hz. This unique approach helps regulate the nervous system's basic functions, supporting improved mental health outcomes.

Key Benefits

Emotional Regulation

Helps stabilize mood and emotional responses

Sleep Improvement

Supports better sleep patterns and quality

Enhanced Focus

Improves attention and concentration

Stress Reduction

Helps manage anxiety and stress levels

How It Works

1

Brain Wave Monitoring

Specialized equipment monitors infra-low frequency brain activity

2

Real-time Feedback

Immediate audio/visual feedback helps train the brain

3

Self-Regulation

The brain learns to optimize its functioning
